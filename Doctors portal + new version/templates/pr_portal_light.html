üìê Roster Generation Rules

These rules are always applied during AI generation to keep things fair and to avoid repeating the same station too often.

  • Clinical and front staff will only be assigned to stations they are allowed to work in.
  • No staff member will be placed in the same station more than twice within a single generated period.
  • AI prefers staff who have worked fewer total days in the range to balance workload.
  • Day-off pools still apply so staff who worked many days in a row get automatic breaks.
These rules are baked into the AI logic in the backend. If you need tighter or looser limits in future (for example, allowing 3 times per station instead of 2), they can be adjusted here and the engine can be updated to follow them.
